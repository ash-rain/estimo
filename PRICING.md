# Estimo - Pricing Strategy

## Overview
Estimo follows a freemium SaaS pricing model with a generous free trial, tiered subscription plans, and usage-based limits. The pricing strategy balances accessibility for small businesses with scalability for enterprise users.

---

## Free Trial

### Trial Details
- **Duration**: 14 days (no credit card required)
- **Plan Access**: Full Professional plan features
- **User Limit**: Up to 3 team members
- **Quote Limit**: Up to 25 quotes
- **Client Limit**: Up to 50 clients
- **Catalog Items**: Up to 200 items

### Trial Features
All Professional plan features included:
- Unlimited quote creation (within trial limits)
- PDF generation and customization
- Client portal access
- Email delivery
- Basic reporting
- Quote templates
- CSV import/export
- Activity logging

### Trial Conversion
- Automatic downgrade to Free plan after 14 days if no subscription
- Email reminders on days 7, 12, and 14
- One-click upgrade option from dashboard
- Data retained for 30 days after trial expiry

## Payment Terms

### Billing Cycles
- **Monthly**: Pay-as-you-go, cancel anytime
- **Annual**: Save 15-20%, paid upfront

### Payment Methods
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit card
- ACH/Bank transfer (Business+ only)
- Invoice/PO (Enterprise only)

### Refund Policy
- 30-day money-back guarantee (first subscription only)
- Prorated refunds for annual plans (minus monthly equivalent)
- No refunds for add-ons or overages

### Cancellation
- Monthly plans: Cancel anytime, access until end of billing period
- Annual plans: No refund, access until end of annual term
- Data export available for 30 days after cancellation
- Account deletion after 90 days of inactivity

## Trial-to-Paid Conversion Strategy

### During Trial
1. **Day 1**: Welcome email with setup guide
2. **Day 3**: Feature highlight email (quote builder)
3. **Day 7**: Halfway reminder + success stories
4. **Day 10**: Advanced features tutorial
5. **Day 12**: Upgrade incentive (first month 20% off)
6. **Day 14**: Final reminder + 24-hour extension offer

### Post-Trial
- Automatic downgrade to Free plan
- Upgrade prompts on feature access
- "Upgrade to unlock" banners
- Usage limit notifications

### Conversion Incentives
- First month 20% off (trial users only)
- Free onboarding call (Professional+)
- Free data migration assistance (Business+)
- Extended trial (additional 7 days) for engaged users

---

## Plan Enforcement

### Hard Limits (Blocking)
- Team member count
- Active quote count (Free plan)
- Client count (Free/Starter)
- Catalog item count (Free/Starter)
- Email sending monthly limit
- File storage limit
- API call daily limit

### Soft Limits (Notifications)
- Approaching storage limit (80%)
- Email quota warning (90%)
- API rate limit warning
- Data retention notice

### Overage Handling
1. **First overage**: Warning email, 3-day grace period
2. **Continued overage**: Auto-charge for overages (if enabled)
3. **Repeated overage**: Suggest plan upgrade
